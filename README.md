# Namma-Skill 🚀
### AI-Powered Vocational Training Platform

Namma-Skill is a modern Android application designed to bridge the gap between rural aspirants and vocational training opportunities. By leveraging Generative AI, the platform helps candidates articulate their potential and track their journey toward professional growth in real-time.

## 🚩 Problem Statement
Rural youth often miss vocational opportunities due to fragmented information and struggle to articulate their professional potential. **Namma-Skill** bridges this gap by providing a centralized course discovery platform and using AI to help students present themselves professionally to enrollment officers.
